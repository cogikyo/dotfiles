# End of Year - Yearly Summary

Aggregate quarterly Reviews into a comprehensive yearly summary.

## Steps

1. **Read all quarterly files:**
   - `~/Documents/life/work/impact/2026/Q1.md`
   - `~/Documents/life/work/impact/2026/Q2.md`
   - `~/Documents/life/work/impact/2026/Q3.md`
   - `~/Documents/life/work/impact/2026/Q4.md`

2. **Synthesize the year's work:**
   - Major projects and their evolution
   - Biggest accomplishments
   - Skills developed
   - Impact on the organization
   - Career growth

3. **Create/update the EOY file** (`~/Documents/life/work/impact/2026/EOY.md`).

## EOY Format

```markdown
# 2026 End of Year Review

## Major Projects
### [Project Name]
Brief description, timeline, impact

## Key Accomplishments
- Accomplishment with context and impact

## Growth & Learning
- Technical skills developed
- Soft skills improved
- Areas of expertise deepened

## Impact Summary
- Quantifiable outcomes where possible
- Team/org contributions

## Reflection
- What worked well
- What to improve
- Focus for next year
```
